About Me

I’m not an artist or a creative—I’m a Designer. I work hard with collaborative teams to solve problems for users, whether it’s a tricky navigation, a hard to find action, or a lack of consistency in the UI. My unique education coupled with my experience with the enterprise means I’m ready to get to work. I use Sketch whenever I can, jumping into Illustrator or Photoshop when needed. I’m comfortable with HTML and CSS, and can handle myself with JavaScript. I love what I do, and look forward to tomorrow’s challenges.
